# Learner Profile
Updated: 2026-03-31

## Learning Style
- Preferred explanation mode: concrete scenarios and analogies (phone call, delivery package)
- Pace: moderate, prefers guided reasoning over direct answers
- Responds best to: scenario-based questions where knowledge is applied practically
- Struggles with: abstract terminology without concrete examples (e.g., QUIC needed a scenario to click)
- Benefits from: step-by-step hints that connect to prior knowledge

## Misconception Patterns
- Tends to underestimate own understanding (self-assessment consistently lower than rubric scores)
- Prefers choosing fewer steps/simpler answers initially (e.g., "2 steps enough for handshake")
- With targeted hints can self-correct effectively
